T and S groceries is Ireland's one of the best supermarket. They operate nationwide delivering the best vegetables, fruits and other products to each and every place in Ireland. We operate both in store collection and delivery.

We strive to give our consumers with affordable, healthy, and sustainable food on a daily basis as a big international store with over 1 million employees, allowing them to enjoy a higher quality of life and a simpler way of life.

Due to shop size and necessity, our grocery stores stock largely food, focusing on higher-margin items like fresh vegetables, fruits, processed food and goodeies for kids, as well as everyday staples. They can be found in crowded city centres, residential areas with small shopping districts, and small towns and villages in rural areas.
